Hate finding a time that works for everyone?
Introducing the new event scheduler, Skedg.

Skedg is synchronized with Google Calendar, and uses your calendar and
our algorithm to find the optimal meeting time for you and your friends.
Best of all, its easy and free to use!

Here's how it works:

1. Create an event and share the link with your friends.
You want to schedule an hour-long event between a certain time frame for a certain set of days. It's that simple.


2. Friends join the event and add conflicts to times they don't like.
All of the recommended times are the best times given your Google calendars.



3. Select a green Skedg button to add that event to everyone's calendars!
Check your Google calendar- your event is there!
